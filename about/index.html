About me

Hey there! My name is Christopher Bradley. I'm an intermediate-level programmer and I develop this site in my free time. I primarily use this site to upload games I've created but I may expand in other directions. Nevertheless, a lot of my games on here are prototypes or demos that have no designated purpose. In time, more thorough projects will be uploaded. I plan to eventually publish my games on steam, though I will keep publishing here from time to time. Web games are great and all, but they just aren't suited for larger projects. I'm still working on my main game, which is far more fleshed out than anything I've posted on here. It will be the first game I actually sell, but shouldn't be all that expensive.
